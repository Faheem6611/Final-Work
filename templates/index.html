About Us

About This Project

We are a group of final year Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) students.As part of our Final Year Project (FYP), we designed and developed the Fake Review Detector, an AI-powered web tool that analyzes the authenticity of product or service reviews using machine learning algorithms.This project was inspired by the increasing influence of online reviews on consumer decisions and the growing challenge of detecting fake or manipulated content. Our goal was to build a lightweight, user-friendly system that empowers users and businesses to make more informed choices.
